The Eplaza Platform as the name implies is a online marketplace which will allow merchants an opportunity to take their businesses online with just a few clicks.

Click on the Image below to visit the site...

Eplaza comes with a fully integrated admin and Content Management system(CMS) as well.

Each merchant account comes with a complete automated accounting system with all the book keeping and record system needed to run from a small busines to a large-Scale Enterprise. It makes provision for tracking sales, shipping, delivery and returns. That's not the best part, it comes with a fully functional payroll system as well as tax filing and returns management.

Don't take my word for it. Click on the image above and see for yourself. I worked with a team of seasoned developers designers and accountants in the course of this project as well as a solid testing and research unit.
